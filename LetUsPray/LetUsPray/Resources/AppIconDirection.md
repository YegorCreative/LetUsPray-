# LetUsPray App Icon Direction

## Color Palette

Use the bold Apple Activity-inspired LetUsPray identity as the app icon foundation:

- Volt Lime: `#B8FF00`
- Hot Pink / Activity Red: `#FF2D55`
- Electric Cyan / Aqua Blue: `#00D8FF`
- Premium Gold: `#D4AF37`
- Deep Graphite Background: `#111217`
- Apple Card Dark Surface: `#1C1C1E`

Avoid the previous turquoise, blue, maroon, cream, and ivory identity as the dominant icon direction.

## Icon Concept

Create a premium spiritual mark that feels like prayer, Scripture, and daily rhythm. The icon should feel memorable at a glance, similar in energy to Apple Fitness activity rings, but calmer and more devotional.

Recommended concept:

- Deep Graphite base.
- A luminous prayer/spark mark in the center.
- Subtle layered glow using Volt Lime, Electric Cyan, and Hot Pink.
- Premium Gold used sparingly as a sacred highlight.
- Rounded, minimal, high-contrast composition.

## Shape Direction

Recommended shape language:

- Rounded square-safe composition with generous breathing room.
- Circular or ring-inspired movement to subtly reference daily prayer progress.
- Soft neon glows that remain clean at small sizes.
- Avoid thin linework that disappears on the Home Screen.
- Use strong silhouette contrast against Deep Graphite.

## Suggested Symbol

Suggested symbolic directions:

- Abstract prayer hands reduced to a simple geometric mark.
- A small cross/spark hybrid that avoids looking like generic medical or church signage.
- A Scripture/book shape with a glowing prayer spark above it.
- A single luminous flame/spark representing daily prayer habit.

Best initial direction:

- Deep Graphite rounded-square background.
- Centered Volt Lime rounded prayer mark.
- Electric Cyan and Hot Pink partial glow rings behind it.
- Tiny Premium Gold highlight near the top-right of the mark.

## Apple Icon Rules Reminder

- Provide final icon artwork as square assets; iOS applies the rounded mask automatically.
- Do not include transparent background in final production icon assets.
- Keep the core mark readable at small sizes.
- Avoid tiny text, excessive detail, or thin strokes.
- Test the icon on light wallpaper, dark wallpaper, and the App Library.
- Make sure the icon remains recognizable at notification and Settings sizes.

## No Text Recommendation

Do not place `LetUsPray`, initials, or any readable text inside the final app icon. Text becomes hard to read at small sizes and usually weakens the premium Apple-style feel. Use a strong symbolic mark instead.
